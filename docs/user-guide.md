# JSW Metal Cost Management System — User Guide

Welcome to the JSW Metal Cost Management System (MCMS). This system allows you to build, preview, and export metal and alloy cost calculation worksheets.

---

## 1. Entering Costing Items

To start a cost calculation:

1. Navigate to the **Costing Workspace** page from the sidebar menu.
2. Select your calculation mode:
   - **Metal Mode**: Cost single metals with various grades.
   - **Alloy Mode**: Create multi-item alloy combinations with specified composition percentages.
3. Click **Add Item** to insert new raw costing lines.
4. Input details:
   - **Metal/Material**: Choose standard JSW metals.
   - **Grade**: Select grades to automatically load master-locked price multipliers.
   - **Quantity (kg)**: Specify target volume.
5. The system will compute base cost breakdowns dynamically in real-time.

---

## 2. Managing Drafts & Approvals

- **Saving a Draft**: Click **Save Draft** at the bottom of the worksheet workspace. Your progress will be saved under the "Drafts" tab of your dashboard.
- **Submitting for Approval**: Click **Submit Worksheets** to request review. Submitted worksheets are locked and visible to administrators and employees for clearance.

---

## 3. Exporting Cost Sheets

You can download formatted cost sheets:

1. Under the **Reports** tab or calculation detail page, click **Export**.
2. Select your preferred format:
   - **PDF Report**: Generates a branded JSW costing sheet with breakdown graphics.
   - **Excel Sheet (XLSX)**: Downloads spreadsheet data containing full item details.
   - **CSV Log**: Fast download of bulk comma-separated values.
